About Joseph

After several years in the marketing field, specifically SEO and ecommerce, I was ready to learn more. I wanted to know what was truly under the hood of these online stores I was working with so that I could add even more value for my employers. The simple html and css that I had mostly learned through Google searches wasn't cutting it anymore.

What I didn't expect was to absolutely fall in love with programming. Once I completed Nucamp's "Web Development Fundamentals Bootcamp", I immediately signed up for their "Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp". I took the knowledge I was gaining in the bootcamp and applied that to Shopify's liquid coding language, so that I could build a wholesale site for my employer. I took what I learned from the backend portion of my bootcamp and built my employer a full stack application capable of handling product warranties. Challenging myself through coding quickly became my new passion.

I seek those challenges through my projects as well as learning new frameworks, libraries, and languages. Very early on in my programming journey, this became clear: solving problems through code isn't something I want to simply add to my resume so I can stand out among other marketers, it is what I want to do with my life.

Node Warranty modal

A full stack application developed to handle warranties. Client is able to make API calls to register a product, view registered product for specific customers, and change status of a warranty. In order to validate registration, serial numbers are moved from a "serial numbers" collection to the "registered products collection" upon registration. The admin panel (pictured) gives authenticated users the ability to see all warranties and change their status from "active" to "inactive". This app is currently running on Heroku.
